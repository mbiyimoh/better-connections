## Summary

- **AI-powered contact research** - Have our AI engine search the web for more information about your contacts
- **Mobile-first experience** - Swipe gestures, pull-to-refresh, and immersive Explore chat
- **Bulk contact management** - Select multiple contacts to delete or manage at once
- **We want your feedback** - New feedback button on every page to share ideas and report bugs

## Details

### AI-powered contact research

Run AI research on any contact to have our engine search the web and fill in missing information. It can discover:

- Current job titles and companies
- Professional background and expertise
- Recent news or notable achievements
- Suggested tags based on what it finds

Review the AI's recommendations and apply the ones you want. All research runs are saved in a collapsible history so you can re-run research anytime for fresh insights.

### Mobile-first experience

Better Contacts now works beautifully on your phone:

- Swipe left on contact cards to quickly delete
- Pull down to refresh your contact list
- Hamburger menu for streamlined navigation
- Card-based layouts optimized for touch
- Immersive Explore chat with contact suggestions in a slide-over panel

Whether you're networking at an event or following up on the go, managing your contacts is effortless from any device.

### Bulk contact management

Managing lots of contacts is easier now:

- Select multiple contacts with checkboxes
- Delete selected contacts in one click
- "Delete All Contacts" option in settings for starting fresh

### We want your feedback

You'll notice a new "Leave Feedback" button in the corner of every page. Use it to:

- Report bugs you encounter
- Suggest features you'd love to see
- Share ideas for improvements

Your input directly shapes what we build next. We read every submission.

### Other improvements

- **iCloud import** - Import contacts directly from .vcf files exported from iCloud or other apps
- **New user onboarding** - Instagram-style story slides introduce the app to first-time users
- **Refreshed brand colors** - Updated gold accent throughout the interface
- **Tag editing** - Edit tag text and category before adding suggestions
